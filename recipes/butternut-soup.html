Butternut Soup

Nothing screams autumn more than a warm and comforting butternut soup

Easy to make, remember to accompany it with bread and cheese for a full meal

Ingredients

  • Butternut
  • 2 onions
  • 1 garlic
  • 500mL of beef stock
  • (optional) Heavy cream
  • (optional) Red pepper berries

Steps

  1. Put the butternut, onions and garlic in a big pot on high heat for 3 minutes
  2. Add the stock, make it boil and then turn the heat to medium-low and let it simmer for 20 min
  3. Mix the whole thing
  4. Add the cream and red pepper berries if you wish
