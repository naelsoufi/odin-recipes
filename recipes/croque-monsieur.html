Croque-Monsieur recipe

Croque-Monsieur

Croque-monsieur is a famous French dish. While its origins are murky, it most likely originated from Paris.

Traditionally cooked in the oven, this one is made in a pan! A great opportunity for students to try it out.

Ingredients

  • Sliced bread
  • Ham
  • Flour
  • Butter (salted)
  • Milk
  • Parmesan or other cheese

Steps

  1. Melt the butter in a pan
  2. Put the flour and whisk
  3. Add the milk
  4. Whisk to make the bechamelle
  5. Add parmesan
  6. Put the mixture on a slice of bread
  7. Add the ham
  8. Close the croque-monsieur
  9. Cook it 3 min each side in a buttered pan
